How to Get There

Northern Monk Brewery

Marshalls Mill The Old Flax Store Marshall St. Leeds, LS11 9YJ

By Train

The Brewery is really easy to get to from Leeds train station. From there it’s either a 10-15 minute walk or a 5 minute taxi ride.
